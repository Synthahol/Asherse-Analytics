About Me

Data drives everything in our world. It is in all devices and a product of all actions. It is the fundamental building blocks of analysis and the deriving of all insights into the future. But there is a catch, and that is the complexity of nuances that are inherent to all data. Time, place, environment, the base whims of human psychology and more all play a factor in the outcomes. This is where I shine. I have a born talent for finding the in-between of all things and accurately connecting the dots across time and space to find the hidden insights and the directions that things are most likely to go. I am highly skilled at understanding the meanings and values behind data points and using them to build accurate pictures. Accuracy = Profit.

Services

My mission is to bring cost savings and revenue generating solutions to my employers by utilizing proven data analysis techniques and technologies and to project those savings and positive cash flow into the future.
